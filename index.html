About Us

In 2001, contractor Joel Cantoran first established JC Properties and Construction. In addition to exceptional customer service, JC Properties and Construction has expertise in all areas of home improvement and has worked with a variety of clients including first time home buyers, investors and partnerships with the City of chicago. No matter the size, speciality, or whether its a multi-unit investment property or your own home, JC Properties and Construction can get it all done.
